StrongHold

A competitive turn-based card game where players strategically deploy and maneuver their cards to protect their own StrongHold (King Card) while aiming to destroy the opponent’s. Each action—placing, moving, or attacking—consumes limited energy points, making every move a tactical decision.

Fully developed the game including:

  • * Complete multiplayer functionality using Photon.
  • * Integrated PlayFab for user authentication, leaderboards, in-game shop, currency/gems, and ranking system.
  • * Built AI opponents with Easy, Medium, and Hard difficulties.
  • * Monetized with Unity Ads
  • * Optimized gameplay using Hashmaps and Unity Profiler.

Engine: Unity | Language: C# | Visual Studio | PlayFab, UnityAds

Platform: Android (Google Play)
