Tower Defense

Pawn Brawls

A strategic tower defense game where players deploy pawns on a chessboard to defend their kingdom and attack the enemy. Power-ups evolve pawns into stronger pieces like knights, rooks, or queens, with some boosting speed.

Developed wave system, grid mechanics, custom win/lose logic, animations, power-up design, and built 6 unique levels.

Engine: Unity | Language: C# | Tools: Visual Studio

Platform: Android (Google Play)
