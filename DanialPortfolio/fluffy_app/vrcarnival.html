VR Casual Game

An immersive low-poly VR carnival featuring multiple interactive mini-games like Bottle Flip, Dart Throw, Balloon Shooter, and Can Toss.

  • Bottle Flip: Test precision and timing by flipping bottles to land upright.
  • Dart Game: Aim and throw darts to pop balloons for points.
  • Balloon Shooter: Use a virtual gun to shoot at floating balloons, enhancing hand-eye coordination.
  • Can Toss: Knock over cans with skillful throws to win virtual prizes.
Platform: VR (Meta Horizon)
Designed and developed the entire experience using XR Toolkit. Implemented physics-based mechanics, interactive gameplay logic, and smooth VR movement. Created intuitive level layouts to enhance player engagement across all mini-games.
