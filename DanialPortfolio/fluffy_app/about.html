About Me

I am Danial Siddiqi a highly motivated and versatile game developer with hands on experience building and publishing games across multiple platforms, including Android and VR. Skilled in developing complex algorithms and integrating third party SDKs such as PlayFab, Photon, and Unity Ads, I’ve developed a wide range of projects—from hypercasual runners to strategic multiplayer games and immersive VR experiences.

Driven by a deep passion for game development, I combine strong programming fundamentals with creative problem-solving to craft engaging, performant, and scalable gameplay systems. Whether working on studio projects or independently publishing titles to the Play Store, I strive to deliver polished, fun, and technically sound experiences.
